Getting Started

Ahoy mateys! Welcome to Castaway Cure: KAPS Pirate Rescue! A mighty mission has been bestowed upon you, and it is up to you, the Pirate Doctor, to rescue your mateys and find a castaway cure before it is too late!


Goal

To save as much shipmates and strangers as possible using your limited resources and found inventory


Inventory

At the start of the game, you are given basic necessities and inventory to help you concoct the perfect cure based on the disease of your patients. You only have a limited amount, so use it wisely. If you run out, you will have to dock on the deserted island to find your required supplies.


Mission

For every mission you complete, you get points. Each mission gives different points.
